Privacy policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect basic usage data when you browse the site, such as your IP address, browser type, and interaction with pages. If you choose to contact us or sign up for updates, we also collect the information you provide voluntarily (such as your name and email address).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to improve site performance, personalize content, and respond to inquiries. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies to analyze traffic and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Some features, such as embedded media and external links, may rely on third-party services that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
